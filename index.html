HDX COVID-19 Map Explorer

Weekly/Daily Snapshot as of 11-10-2020

This report provides a ranking for the spread of COVID-19 and related indicators for the 63 countries covered in the Global Humanitarian Response Plan (GHRP). Data is provided by multiple sources through HDX and is updated on a rolling basis. For this reporting period, there was a rise in new COVID-19 cases in 25 out of the 63 HRP countries.
