This on-demand daily provides a set of key indicators related to the pandemic environment in the 25 countries with a Humanitarian Response Plan (HRP). Data is updated as it becomes available and managed by the Humanitarian Data Exchange (HDX) of OCHAâ€™s Centre for Humanitarian Data.

Several criteria and indicative thresholds have been developed to assess the spread of the virus, including related to cases, deaths, and positive test rate. For instance, combined with comprehensive surveillance and testing, a positive test rate of less than 5% is one indicator that transmission may be under control. The indicators highlighted here may vary as the context evolves.

Vaccination Campaigns Delayed represents the percentage of vaccination campaigns that have been postponed or cancelled; Food Market Prices In Crisis represents the percentage of commodities in alert, stress, or crisis.
