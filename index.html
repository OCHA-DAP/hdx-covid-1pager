HDX Covid-19 Data Explorer: On-Demand Daily Snapshot

October 18th 2020

This on-demand daily report provides an overview of the Global Humanitarian Response Plan (GHRP) countries (63 in total) that are most affected by Covid-19. Data is provided by multiple sources through HDX and is updated on a rolling basis.

The 15 GHRP countries with the highest number of new Covid-19 cases per 100,000 people

Country New Cases per 100K,
last 7 days		 New Cases,
last 7 days		 Deaths,
last 7 days		 Positive test rate Vaccination Campaign Status Food Market Prices GHRP Funding

Positive Testing Rate: This is the daily positive rate, given as a rolling 7-day average. According WHO, a positive rate of less than 5% is one indicator that the epidemic is under control in a country. Vaccination campaigns disrupted: This percentage has been determined by calculating the ratio of total number of postponed or cancelled campaigns and total vaccination campaigns. Food Market Prices: This information has been determined by calculating the ratio of the number of commodities in alert, stress or crisis and the total number of commodities.

Sources: WHO (Covid-19 cases and deaths); Our World in Data (Covid-19 testing, Positive testing rate); OCHA (Funding); WFP (Food Prices)

To explore the data for all 63 countries, visit https://data.humdata.org/visualization/covid19-humanitarian-operations/.
